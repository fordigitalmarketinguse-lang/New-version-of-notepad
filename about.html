About Web Notepad

Web Notepad is a free, full-featured online text editor designed to provide the complete functionality of a desktop notepad application directly in your web browser. Our mission is to offer a convenient, accessible, and privacy-focused text editing solution for users across all devices.

Our Story

Web Notepad was created in response to the growing need for simple yet powerful text editing tools that work seamlessly across different platforms. While many text editors require downloads, installations, or subscriptions, we believe that basic text editing should be freely accessible to everyone without barriers.

Our development team consists of software engineers and UX designers who are passionate about creating tools that enhance productivity while respecting user privacy. We've combined the familiar interface of traditional notepad applications with modern web technologies to deliver a robust editing experience.

Privacy Policy

At Web Notepad, we take your privacy seriously. Our application is designed with a fundamental principle: your data belongs to you, and only you.

Data Collection: Web Notepad does not collect, store, or transmit any of your text content to our servers. All processing occurs locally within your web browser. We don't use tracking cookies or analytics that monitor your typing content.

Information Storage: Your text is temporarily stored in your browser's local storage to enable features like auto-recovery and session persistence. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services: We do not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your content. The application is entirely self-contained within your browser environment.

Changes to This Policy: Any updates to our privacy policy will be clearly communicated on this page. We encourage users to periodically review this section for the latest information on our privacy practices.

How It Works

Web Notepad utilizes modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript to provide a desktop-like experience in your browser. The application leverages the File API for opening and saving files, and localStorage for maintaining your content between sessions.

All processing occurs client-side, meaning your documents never leave your computer unless you explicitly choose to save them to your device or print them. This architecture ensures maximum privacy and security for your content.

Technical Information

Web Notepad supports a wide range of technical features including:

  • Multiple text encodings (ANSI, UTF-8, UTF-16)
  • Line ending conversion between Windows, Unix, and Mac formats
  • Comprehensive find and replace functionality
  • Font customization and zoom controls
  • Print formatting options
  • Responsive design for all screen sizes

Contact Information

For questions, suggestions, or support regarding Web Notepad, please visit our feedback page within the application. We value user input and continuously work to improve our tool based on community feedback.

Thank you for choosing Web Notepad as your text editing solution. We're committed to providing a reliable, feature-rich, and privacy-conscious editor for all your note-taking and document creation needs.
